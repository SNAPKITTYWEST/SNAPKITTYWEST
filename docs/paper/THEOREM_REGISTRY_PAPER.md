## 1. Scope and Method

All theorems below are written in **Lean 4** and live inside the SNAPKITTYWEST
workspace and the SnapKitty Agent OS. The registry was generated by reading the
twelve `.lean` files directly; every line number is taken from the source as it
stands in the repository on 2026-07-08.

**Totals:** 78 theorems · 63 proven (sorry-free) · 15 with `sorry` (admitted) ·
11 axioms.

A theorem marked **PROVEN** has a terminating constructive proof. A theorem
marked **SORRY** has its statement declared but its proof body is the Lean
placeholder `sorry` — i.e. the result is claimed but not yet formally closed. An
**AXIOM** is a foundational assumption accepted without proof (used where a
result is imported from outside Lean's kernel or is independently known).
